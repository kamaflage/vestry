# Vestry — Household Budget

A private household budget planner. No account, no cloud, no ads.

## What it is
Vestry is a single-file web app for tracking household income, expenses, savings goals, and recurring bills. It runs entirely in your browser — all data stays on your device.

## Features
- Income, expense, savings, and one-off transaction tracking
- Monthly budget targets with progress tracking
- Budget health score
- Savings goals
- Recurring bills registry
- Partner sharing and merge (no server needed)
- Works offline
- Installable as a home screen app on Android

## Privacy
No data is ever sent anywhere. Everything is stored in your browser's local storage.
